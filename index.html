FAQ

Questions Sellers Usually Ask

If your question isn't here, just send a message — I'm happy to answer directly.

What services do you offer?

I offer Amazon account management, PPC campaign setup and optimisation, listing creation and optimisation, keyword research, competitor analysis, product research, and inventory creation and management. Most clients combine several services depending on their needs and stage of growth.

I'm based outside the UK — can you still help me?

Absolutely. The majority of my clients are international sellers — brands based in the Middle East, South Asia, Europe and North America selling on multiple Amazon marketplaces. I work across time zones and communicate via email and WhatsApp.

Is SellerBeacon an agency or an individual consultant?

SellerBeacon is a solo consultancy. You work directly with me — not a junior account manager or a rotating team. This means consistent communication, genuine accountability and no "lost in translation" between who promises results and who actually does the work.

How quickly can I expect to see results?

It depends on the service. PPC restructures typically show measurable improvement within 3–6 weeks. Listing optimisations can impact conversion rates within 30–60 days. Organic ranking improvements take longer — usually 60–90 days or more depending on competition. I'll give you realistic timelines for your specific situation from the outset.

Do you work with new Amazon sellers or only established brands?

Both. For new sellers, I can help with account setup, initial listing creation, keyword research and launch PPC strategy. For established brands, I focus on optimisation, scaling ad spend efficiently and fixing issues that are limiting growth. The discovery call helps us understand which approach fits your situation.

What marketplaces do you cover?

I have direct experience managing accounts on Amazon USA, UK, Canada, Germany, France, Italy, Japan, Spain and UAE. If you're targeting a marketplace not listed, get in touch and we can discuss whether it's something I can support.
